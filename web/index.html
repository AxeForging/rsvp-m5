What it does A focused reader that stays out of your way
Read One word at a time

Words flash in place so your eyes never move. Adjustable speed, a focus highlight, and dyslexia-friendly fonts.

Control Tap to play or pause

A quick tap anywhere plays or pauses; on-screen zones and the front buttons handle speed. Nothing fiddly to learn.

Connect Keeps your phone online

Set up your home Wi-Fi once and the reader joins it — your phone keeps its internet while you send books across.

Sync Wireless library

Upload books and articles, adjust settings, and manage RSS feeds from any browser. No app to install.

Convert Bring any book

Turn EPUB, TXT, Markdown, or HTML into reader files right in your browser, or copy them straight to the SD card.

Maintain Updates over the air

Once Wi-Fi is set, the reader can update its own firmware — no cable needed.

The recommended path is to convert supported source books in the browser, then place the generated .rsvp files in the SD card’s /books/books folder.

  1. Insert the SD card into the reader.
  2. Open USB transfer on the device, or remove the card and use a card reader.
  3. Pick the SD card’s /books/books folder in the workspace below, then import, clean sidecars, and sync.

The firmware can read .rsvp, .txt, and .epub files from /books/books, /books/articles, and files directly under /books, but browser-converted .rsvp files are the best-supported book format.

USB Transfer Expose the SD card from the device menu

On the default USB-enabled firmware, open USB transfer from the main menu to expose the SD card over USB. When you finish copying files, eject the device from your computer, then hold PWR to leave transfer mode.

Folder Layout Keep book files under /books/books

The workspace below can sync directly into the books folder. Put saved articles in /books/articles so they appear on the device’s Articles page.

Cleanup Remove metadata and interrupted sidecars

The Clean Sidecars action helps clear platform metadata and temporary files such as .rsvp.tmp, .rsvp.converting, and .rsvp.failed.

Put your SD card in a reader, then Connect SD Card and pick its /books/books folder for one-click import, cleanup, and sync. No card reader? Add books from your computer and download the .rsvp files instead. Everything is converted locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Don’t want to keep pulling the SD card? Open Sync on the device (Menu or top-edge swipe), join its RSVP-M5 Wi-Fi network, and upload the .rsvp files wirelessly — no cable, no card removal.

    Browser Not Supported

    The firmware installer requires the Web Serial API, available in Chrome or Edge on macOS, Windows, Linux, or ChromeOS.

    Safari, Firefox, and all mobile browsers do not currently support Web Serial. You can still use the Library Workspace below to convert books.
