RSVP M5 v0.0.8 — reads and speaks your language.

## What's new

- **Menus in your language.** The full on-device UI — main menu, settings, and their values — now
  follows your reading language across Japanese, Chinese, Korean, and the European languages. The
  Language setting itself always stays in English so you can always find your way back.
- **Switch language without reflashing.** Change the reading language any time from
  Settings → Language. If you pick a script this build didn't bundle, the reader offers to download
  the full font over Wi-Fi and applies it on the spot — no cable, no unreadable screens.
- **Cleaner reading view.** Swipe to the scroll view for a clean, full-page read with the chapter,
  battery, and progress chrome out of the way — CJK included.
- **Snappier touch.** Faster, more responsive taps and swipes; swipe right to enter and left to go
  back in menus. Long scrub swipes move by more words at once.
- **Lighter and quicker under the hood.** A faster display path and idle-time yield keep things
  smooth and easy on the battery.

## Install

Per-language builds for the M5Stack Core2 — each bundles only its own reading font, so pick your
language in the web flasher. `en` is the lightest; the full multi-script Noto font downloads on
demand from the device.

- `rsvp-m5-core2-<lang>.bin` — full browser-flasher image for a fresh install (`<lang>` in en/ja/zh/ko).
- `rsvp-m5-core2-<lang>-ota.bin` — matching over-the-air update image.
- `rsvp-m5-cjk.vlw` — complete Noto CJK font, downloaded on demand for full coverage.
