RSVP M5 v0.0.3 — a friendlier reader.

## What's new

- **Tap to play or pause.** A quick tap anywhere on the screen starts and stops reading, instantly.
- **Easier speed control.** On-screen zones along the bottom edge change speed, alongside the front buttons — and the speed readout now stays on screen long enough to read.
- **Single-purpose buttons.** Left slows down, center plays/pauses, right speeds up. Nothing hidden on long presses.
- **Keeps your phone online.** Sync now joins your home Wi-Fi so your phone keeps its internet while you send books across.
- **Guided Wi-Fi setup.** First-time setup walks you through connecting — on the reader, or from the companion page — so the hotspot stops dropping your phone.
- **Refreshed companion page.** The wireless upload and settings page has a cleaner look.

## Install

- `rsvp-m5-core2.bin` — full browser-flasher image for a fresh install on the M5Stack Core2.
- `rsvp-m5-core2-ota.bin` — over-the-air update image.
